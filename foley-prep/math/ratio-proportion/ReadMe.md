## Percent Practice Problems

Here are 5 problems focusing on various percent concepts, including increases, decreases, and finding parts of a whole.

### 1. Simple Percent of a Number

A local library has 1,200 books. If 35% of the books are non-fiction, how many non-fiction books does the library have?

### 2. Percent Increase

A pair of sneakers originally cost $80. The price was increased by 15% due to high demand. What is the new price of the sneakers?

### 3. Percent Decrease (Sale)

A laptop is on sale for 20% off its original price of $950. What is the sale price of the laptop before tax?

### 4. Finding the Percent

A student answered 42 out of 50 questions correctly on a math test. What percentage did the student receive on the test?

### 5. Reverse Percent (Finding the Whole)

A real estate agent earned a 3% commission on the sale of a house. If the agent's commission was $9,000, what was the total selling price of the house?
