# Ratios/Proportions - Fractions

1. **The Pizza Party:** A large pizza is cut into **12 equal slices**. If a group of friends eats $\frac{3}{4}$ of the pizza, how many slices are left over?
2. **The Chocolate Bar:** A chocolate bar is divided into **15 identical squares**. If Leo eats all **but** $\frac{1}{5}$ of the bar, how many squares did he eat?
3. **The Box of Cookies:** There are **24 cookies** in a box. Sarah eats $\frac{1}{6}$ of the cookies in the morning and $\frac{2}{6}$ of the cookies in the afternoon. How many total cookies did she eat?
4. **The Garden Plot:** A rectangular garden is divided into **10 equal sections**. If a gardener plants flowers in $\frac{2}{5}$ of the sections and vegetables in $\frac{1}{2}$ of the sections, how many sections are planted in total?
5. **The Dozen Cupcakes:** A bakery box holds a **dozen (12) cupcakes**. If a customer buys all **but** $\frac{1}{4}$ of the cupcakes, how many cupcakes did they buy?
6. **The Ribbon Roll:** A roll of ribbon is **20 feet long**. A crafter uses $\frac{2}{10}$ of the roll for one project and $\frac{5}{10}$ for another. How many feet of ribbon were used in total?
7. **The Fruit Basket:** A basket contains **18 pieces of fruit**. If the family eats all **but** $\frac{1}{3}$ of the fruit, how many pieces of fruit did they eat?
